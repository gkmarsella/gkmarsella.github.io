About

After 3 years of working as an Arborist, I broke my elbow while on the job. During my recovery, I challenged myself to learn Javascript and HTML/CSS. As it turned out, I found coding so enjoyable that I wanted to learn more about full-stack development and ultimately make a career out of it. Later that year, I attended Rithm School's' four month web development program in San Francisco to expand my skill set and gain more experience architecting apps. The curriculum covered both front and back end development, including Javascript, jQuery, HTML, CSS, Python, Flask, SQL, and React.

Since graduation, I have continued to learn new technologies and contribute to projects.
